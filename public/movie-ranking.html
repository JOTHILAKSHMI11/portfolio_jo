The Best Movies According to Jo!

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Shawshank Redemption

A beautiful, inspiring story about hope, friendship, and patience. It teaches us that the human spirit can never be truly imprisoned.

The Godfather

The ultimate masterpiece on power, loyalty, and family. It perfectly balances incredible acting with a deep dive into the corruption of a crime family.

Parasite

A brilliantly written, suspenseful thriller. It cleverly explores wealth gaps and social classes while keeping you entirely glued to the screen.
